# Product Guidelines - Foosball Statistics Platform

## Visual Identity
- **Vibe:** Playful & Energetic. The design should feel dynamic and exciting, mirroring the fast-paced nature of the game and serving as a mental break from work.
- **Palette:** Use high-contrast, vibrant colors to signify teams and game states.

## Prose & Tone
- **Voice:** Casual, Friendly, and Enthusiastic.
    - *Example:* "Awesome game! 🚀 You just climbed past [Name] on the leaderboard!"
    - *Avoid:* Dry, bureaucratic language like "Match submission processed successfully."

## User Experience (UX) Core Philosophy
- **Dual-Context Design:**
    - **Match Entry (Mobile):** "Speed & Thumb-Friendly." Optimized for quick, few-click entry on mobile devices while standing at the table.
    - **Analysis (Desktop):** "Deep & Detailed." The desktop view is the command center for exploring complex statistics and leaderboards comfortably.

## Notification Strategy
- **Critical & Ranking-Based:**
    - **Essential:** Immediate alerts for Match Confirmations and Disputes.
    - **Competitive (Targeted):** "Rating Change" alerts are exclusive to the "Best Players" tables (Most Matches Won & Most Games Won). We avoid noise for minor stat fluctuations.
- **Real-Time Matchmaking:**
    - **"Ready to Play" Pool:** Users can signal availability for 1v1 or 2v2.
    - **Pool Alerts:** Optional opt-in for "New Player Joined" alerts; Mandatory alert when a match pool is full (e.g., 4 players ready for doubles).
